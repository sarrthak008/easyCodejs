today we are learn about console

1] console.log()  : - use to log the output 
  eg: console.log("log the output")

2] console.info() :- print info on the console
  eg : console.info("print info on the console")

3] console.warn() :- print warning on the console
  eg : console.warn("print warning on the console")

4] console.error() :- print error on the console
  eg : console.error("print error on the console")

5] console.clear() :- clear the console
  eg : console.clear()
